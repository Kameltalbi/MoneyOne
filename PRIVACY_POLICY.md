# Privacy Policy for MoneyOne

**Last updated: February 20, 2026**

## Introduction

MoneyOne ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
MoneyOne stores all your financial data **locally on your device**:
- Transactions (income and expenses)
- Accounts and balances
- Budgets and savings goals
- Categories and recurring transactions

**This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the backup feature.**

### Data Collected by Third Parties

#### Google Play Billing
- When you purchase a Pro subscription, Google Play processes your payment
- We receive only confirmation of your subscription status (active/inactive)
- We do NOT receive your payment information

#### Firebase (Optional)
- If you use cloud backup, Firebase stores your encrypted data
- Anonymous usage analytics (crash reports)
- No personal identification information is collected

#### Google Drive (Optional)
- If you enable Google Drive backup, your data is stored in your personal Google Drive
- We only access files created by MoneyOne
- You can revoke access at any time

## Permissions We Use

### Required Permissions
- **INTERNET**: Required for Pro subscription verification and optional cloud backup
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: To check network connectivity before sync

### Optional Permissions
- **CAMERA**: Only used when you scan receipts (OCR feature)
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: To send budget alerts and reminders

## How We Use Your Data

- **Local Processing**: All financial calculations are done on your device
- **No Selling**: We NEVER sell your data to third parties
- **No Ads**: MoneyOne contains no advertising
- **No Tracking**: We don't track your behavior or location

## Data Security

- All data is stored in encrypted SQLite database
- Cloud backups are encrypted before upload
- No data is transmitted without your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data in the app
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
- **Export**: Export your data to CSV/PDF
- **Revoke**: Disconnect Google Drive access at any time

## Children's Privacy

MoneyOne is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.

- CCPA (California)
